    Australia has hundreds of CRICOS registered education providers that are authorised to deliver courses to international students studying on a student visa. These providers include universities, vocational colleges, English language schools and specialist training institutions located across major Australian study destinations such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide.

    This page helps you explore CRICOS providers in Australia and discover the institutions offering active programs in different fields of study. Each provider page includes the official CRICOS provider code, active course count and locations where programs are delivered.
