    About CRICOS Finder

    CRICOS Finder is a simple search tool designed to help international students explore CRICOS registered courses, education providers and study locations across Australia.

    The platform organizes official CRICOS data into easy-to-navigate pages so students can quickly find courses, compare providers and discover study opportunities in different Australian cities.

    What is CRICOS?

    CRICOS stands for the Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students.

    It is the official Australian government register that lists education providers and courses approved to deliver programs to international students studying on a student visa.

    Only courses and institutions listed on CRICOS are authorised to enrol overseas students in Australia.

    What CRICOS Finder does

    CRICOS Finder simplifies the process of exploring CRICOS registered courses and education providers. Instead of navigating complex government databases, the platform presents the information in clear, structured pages that allow students to browse courses by provider, city or study area.

    What you can do on CRICOS Finder

    • Search courses by city, provider or keyword
    • Browse education providers and view their registered courses
    • Explore study options in different Australian cities
    • Discover courses by study area or field

    Data coverage

    CRICOS Finder indexes publicly available information from the official CRICOS register.

    The dataset includes:

    • CRICOS registered education providers
    • approved courses for international students
    • campus locations and study cities
    • provider codes and course codes

    The information is organized into structured pages that help users quickly navigate between providers, courses and study destinations.

    Who this site is for

    CRICOS Finder is designed primarily for international students who want to explore study opportunities in Australia. The platform can also be useful for education agents, researchers and anyone looking to better understand the structure of CRICOS registered courses and providers across the country.

    Important note

    CRICOS Finder displays information for reference and discovery purposes only. While the data is sourced from official CRICOS records, students should always verify key details directly with the education provider or the official Australian Government CRICOS website before applying for a course.
