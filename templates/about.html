    About CRICOS Finder

    CRICOS Finder is an independent search platform designed to help international students explore CRICOS-registered education providers, courses, and study locations across Australia.

    The website organizes publicly available CRICOS data into structured and easy-to-navigate pages, allowing users to quickly search courses, compare education providers, and explore study opportunities in different Australian cities.

    What is CRICOS?

    CRICOS stands for the Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students.

    It is the official Australian government register that lists education providers and courses approved to deliver education programs to international students studying on an Australian student visa.

    Only institutions and courses listed in the CRICOS register are authorised to enrol overseas students in Australia.

    Purpose of CRICOS Finder

    The official CRICOS register contains a large amount of information about courses, institutions, and campus locations. However, the official database can be difficult to explore for users who are simply trying to compare study options.

    CRICOS Finder was created to simplify access to this information by organizing CRICOS data into clear and searchable pages.

    Users can browse:

    • CRICOS-registered education providers
    • courses approved for international students
    • study areas and academic fields
    • Australian cities where courses are delivered

    This structure helps users quickly navigate the available education options.

    Who this website is for

    CRICOS Finder is primarily designed for international students researching study opportunities in Australia.

    The platform may also be useful for:

    • education agents
    • researchers studying the Australian education system
    • prospective students comparing institutions and study programs

    Data Sources

    CRICOS Finder indexes publicly available information from the official CRICOS register maintained by the Australian Government.

    The dataset includes:

    • CRICOS registered education providers
    • approved courses for international students
    • campus locations and study cities
    • provider codes and course codes

    For detailed information about the data source and processing methods, please see:

    • Data Source
    • Methodology

    Important Notice

    CRICOS Finder is an independent informational website and is not affiliated with the Australian Government.

    The platform is designed for discovery and research purposes only. Course availability, tuition fees, program duration, and provider status may change over time. Before applying for a course, students should always verify important details directly with the education provider or through the official CRICOS register.
