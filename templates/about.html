    About CRICOS Finder

    CRICOS Finder is built for one simple task: help students find the right CRICOS course, city and provider faster.

    What you can do

    • Enter a city and see available courses and locations in that city.
    • Enter a course and see in which cities and providers it is offered.
    • Open a provider page and view its courses, provider code and locations.

    Data scope

    The index is based on CRICOS providers, courses and campus locations. Results are grouped to show cities, location names, course titles and provider codes in one clear table.

    SEO and usability goal

    Make CRICOS search simple, fast and readable from any country, especially where official sites are slow or hard to use.
