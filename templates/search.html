    Australia offers thousands of CRICOS registered courses for international students across universities, colleges and training institutions. These programs are officially approved to be delivered to overseas students studying on an Australian student visa.

    CRICOS courses cover a wide range of fields including business, information technology, engineering, nursing, hospitality, English language training and community services. Programs are available in major Australian study destinations such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide, as well as many regional cities.

    The CRICOS Finder search tool helps students explore these opportunities by organizing official CRICOS data into searchable course listings. You can browse programs by provider, compare courses by city, or discover study options within specific academic fields.

    Each course listed on CRICOS Finder includes important reference information such as the course name, CRICOS code, education provider and study location. These details help students identify programs that are approved for international enrolment in Australia.

    While CRICOS Finder makes it easier to discover available courses, students should always confirm key details directly with the education provider or through the official government CRICOS register before submitting an application.

    FAQ

    What are CRICOS courses?

    CRICOS courses are programs offered by Australian education providers that are officially approved to be delivered to international students on a student visa.

    Can international students study any course in Australia?

    International students must enroll in a course that is listed on the Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students (CRICOS).

    How can I search for CRICOS courses?

    You can search CRICOS courses by provider, city, study area or course name using the CRICOS Finder search tool.
