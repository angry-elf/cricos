    Australia is one of the most popular study destinations for international students. Many universities, colleges and training providers across the country offer CRICOS-registered courses that allow overseas students to study on a student visa.

    This page lists some of the most popular Australian cities where international students choose to study. Each city page shows available CRICOS courses, education providers and campus locations where programs are delivered.

    Major study destinations such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane and Perth host hundreds of education providers and thousands of courses across fields like business, information technology, nursing, engineering and English language training. Smaller cities and regional study destinations also offer quality education options and can provide different lifestyle and cost-of-living advantages.
