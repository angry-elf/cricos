What is CRICOS?

CRICOS (Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students) is the official Australian register of education providers and courses available to overseas students studying in Australia on student visas. This site helps you explore that information in a more convenient and searchable format.

Education providers must be registered in CRICOS to enrol overseas students.
