    Contact CRICOS Finder

    CRICOS Finder is an independent project that helps users explore publicly available information about CRICOS-registered education providers, courses, and study locations in Australia.

    If you notice incorrect information, have suggestions for improving the site, or would like to ask a general question about the project, you can contact us.

    Typical contact reasons include:

    • reporting incorrect provider or course information
    • suggesting improvements to the search system
    • general feedback about the website
    • technical issues

    Please note that CRICOS Finder cannot provide visa, migration, or education advice.

    For official information about education providers or courses, please contact the provider directly or refer to the official CRICOS register.

    Email contact:
    contact@cricos.net
