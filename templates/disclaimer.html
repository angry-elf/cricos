    Disclaimer

    CRICOS Finder is an independent informational website designed to help users explore publicly available information about CRICOS-registered education providers, courses, study areas, and locations in Australia.

    The website is not an official government website and is not affiliated with the Australian Government or the official CRICOS register.

    CRICOS Finder aggregates publicly available data to make it easier for international students and researchers to search and compare education providers, courses, and study destinations.

    While we aim to present information in a clear and useful way, course availability, tuition fees, program duration, campus locations, and provider status may change at any time.

    Users should always verify important details directly with the official CRICOS register or with the relevant education provider before applying for a course or making decisions related to study or visa applications.

    CRICOS Finder does not provide legal, migration, visa, or educational advice. All information presented on this website is for general informational purposes only.
