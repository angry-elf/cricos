    Australia offers thousands of CRICOS-registered courses across many different study areas. Universities, vocational colleges and English language schools provide programs designed for international students who want to study in Australia on a student visa.

    Popular study areas include business, information technology, engineering, nursing, hospitality and community services. These programs are available in major Australian study destinations such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide, as well as in regional education hubs.
