    Methodology

    CRICOS Finder processes publicly available CRICOS records into searchable pages by provider, city and study area.

    1. Import the latest available CRICOS dataset.
    2. Normalize providers, courses and campus locations.
    3. Generate searchable pages and internal links for city, provider and course intent.
    4. Display data as reference content and link users to official sources for final checks.
