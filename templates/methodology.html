    CRICOS Finder Methodology

    CRICOS Finder organizes publicly available CRICOS data into structured and searchable pages that help international students explore study opportunities in Australia.

    The platform processes official CRICOS datasets and converts them into easy-to-navigate pages for courses, providers, cities and study areas.

    Data Source

    All information displayed on CRICOS Finder is derived from publicly available CRICOS records provided by the Australian Government. CRICOS (Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students) is the official registry of Australian education providers and courses approved to deliver programs to international students on a student visa.

    To ensure accuracy, CRICOS Finder imports the latest available dataset and uses it as the primary source for course, provider and campus information.

    Data Processing

    After importing the CRICOS dataset, the data is processed and structured into several components:

    • education providers
    • registered courses
    • campus locations
    • study areas and course categories

    During this step the system standardizes provider names, course titles and location data to improve consistency and searchability across the website. Duplicate or inconsistent location formats are normalized so that users can easily browse courses by city or study destination.

    Page Generation

    CRICOS Finder automatically generates structured pages that allow users to explore the dataset through multiple navigation paths. The platform creates searchable pages for:

    • individual education providers
    • Australian cities and study destinations
    • study areas and course fields
    • course search results and listings

    These pages are internally linked so users can move easily between providers, courses, cities and study areas.

    Search and Discovery

    The search system allows visitors to find CRICOS-registered courses and providers using different types of queries. Users can search by:

    • course name
    • provider name
    • CRICOS course code
    • provider code
    • study field or qualification level
    • city or study destination

    This flexible search structure helps international students compare study options across Australia.

    Data Usage and Limitations

    CRICOS Finder displays data for informational and discovery purposes. While the information is sourced from official CRICOS records, users should always verify critical details directly with the education provider or the official government CRICOS website before applying.

    Course availability, campus locations and provider details may change over time as institutions update their CRICOS registrations.
