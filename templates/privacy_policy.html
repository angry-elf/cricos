    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: 6 March 2026

    CRICOS Finder ("we", "our", or "the site") operates the website https://cricos.net. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when visitors use the website and how that information is used.

    CRICOS Finder provides searchable information about CRICOS-registered education providers, courses, study areas, and cities in Australia. The platform is designed to help international students explore education opportunities and understand where specific courses are offered.

    Information We Collect

    When users browse or search the website, limited information may be collected automatically. This information may include:

    • Search queries entered in the city or course search fields
    • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and operating system
    • Pages visited and navigation patterns within the website
    • Anonymous analytics data related to site performance and user interaction

    CRICOS Finder does not require user accounts and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

    How We Use the Information

    The information collected is used primarily to operate and improve the website. This may include:

    • Displaying relevant search results for CRICOS courses, providers, and locations
    • Improving the accuracy and usability of search features
    • Monitoring site performance and technical reliability
    • Identifying potential technical issues or misuse of the platform
    • Understanding general usage patterns to improve content and navigation

    Information collected through analytics is used only in aggregated or anonymized form.

    Cookies

    Cookies may be used to support core website functionality and analytics.

    Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website and allow certain features, such as search functionality and performance monitoring, to work effectively.

    Users can disable or control cookies through their browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

    Analytics and Third-Party Services

    The website may rely on third-party infrastructure or analytics services to operate effectively.

    These services may process limited technical data such as IP addresses, device information, or website usage statistics. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

    CRICOS Finder does not sell or trade user data.

    Data Storage and Security

    We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the systems used to operate the website.

    However, no internet-based service can guarantee complete security. Users should understand that any online interaction carries some level of risk.

    Data Retention

    Technical logs and analytics information may be stored for a limited time for the purposes of system monitoring, troubleshooting, and service improvement.

    Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operational and security purposes.

    External Links

    CRICOS Finder may include links to external websites, including official Australian government resources and education provider websites.

    These external websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the content or data practices of third-party websites.

    Informational Nature of the Website

    CRICOS Finder provides aggregated and searchable reference information related to CRICOS-registered courses and providers.

    While the website aims to present accurate and useful information, users should verify important details with official government sources or education providers before making decisions related to study or visa applications.

    Updates to This Privacy Policy

    This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the website or legal requirements. The most recent version will always be published on this page together with the date of the latest update.

    Contact

    If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the operation of the website, please contact the site operator using the Contact page available on this website.
